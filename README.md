### HackIllinois 2026 — CatSense (Caterpillar Track)
> *Multimodal AI for heavy machinery inspection — built in 36 hours*

Equipment inspection tool that takes technicians from 40-minute paper
forms to under 10 minutes. Photo + audio → Gemini 2.5 Flash → structured
report with severity ratings and citations from CAT service manuals.
Three-layer hallucination control because safety reports have to mean
what they say.

Stack: React + TypeScript · Gemini 2.5 Flash · Actian VectorAI (RAG) · FastAPI · Cloudflare Workers · Zod

[→ Devpost](https://devpost.com/software/catsense-coq2af) · [→ Repo](https://github.com/yachi2605/CatSense)
